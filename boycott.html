Boycott Citizens Bank

Why boycott Citizens Bank?

  • Susceptible to public opinion: Citizens Bank is a consumer-facing brand that cares about its reputation and is more likely to respond to public pressure than private prison companies or government agencies.
  • Important to ICE: Citizens Bank provides financial services to companies that operate ICE detention centers. Cutting off this support can disrupt ICE's ability to detain immigrants.
